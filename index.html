resists.org

This domain provides subdomains for regional Ingress Resistance groups. Generally, we try to follow craigslist naming for easy discovery. For example, "sfbay.craigslist.org" becomes "sfbay.resists.org".

The individual groups are run by their local communities.

Current groups:

  • San Francisco Bay Area
  • Hawaii
  • Sacramento
  • New York City

Additional player resources:

  • Official Ingress Support
  • Google Hangouts Help

If you would like a resists.org subdomain for your community, please email balshor@resists.org. While we do not host community websites ourselves, we can provide some pointers for how and where to set up your site.
